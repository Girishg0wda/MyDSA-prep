# Two Pointers

## What is the Two Pointers Technique?

The Two Pointers technique uses two indices to traverse a data structure, usually from opposite ends.

Example:

Array: [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]

left = 0
right = 4

## Why Use It?

Instead of using nested loops (O(n²)), many problems can be solved in O(n).

## Common Problems

* Pair Sum
* Reverse String
* Palindrome Check
* Remove Duplicates
* Container With Most Water

## Complexity

Time Complexity: O(n)

Space Complexity: O(1)

## Key Idea

Move pointers based on conditions until they meet.
